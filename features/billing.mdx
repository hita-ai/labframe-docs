# Billing

LabFrame uses a credit-based system. Every AI interaction — messages, tool usage, and compute — consumes credits from your account balance.

## Plans

LabFrame offers several plan tiers:

| Plan | Monthly Credits | Best For |
|------|:-:|---------|
| **Starter** | 2,000 | Individual students and light usage |
| **Pro** | 4,000 | Active students and researchers |
| **Team** | 20,000 | Research labs and course sections |
| **Enterprise** | 50,000 | Departments and large deployments |

## Credit usage

Credits are consumed by:

- **Chat messages** — each message to the agent uses credits based on the model and response length
- **Tool execution** — running commands, browsing the web, and file operations
- **Compute time** — the agent's cloud workspace incurs a small per-minute cost while active

Your current balance and recent usage are visible in **Settings > Billing**.

## Top-ups

If you run out of credits before your monthly renewal, you can purchase one-time credit packs:

| Pack | Credits |
|------|:-:|
| Small | 5,000 |
| Medium | 10,000 |
| Large | 20,000 |

## Low balance alerts

LabFrame warns you when your credit balance is running low. If your balance reaches zero, your agents pause until credits are replenished (either through a top-up or your next billing cycle).

## Managing billing

Go to **Settings > Billing** to:

- View your current plan and balance
- See recent credit usage
- Purchase top-ups
- Manage your subscription through the Stripe customer portal
