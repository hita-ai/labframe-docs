---
title: Sharing & Inviting
description: Invite collaborators to work with your agent in real time.
---

LabFrame lets you share conversations so multiple people can interact with the same agent, see the same workspace, and contribute to the same thread.

## Sharing a project

To invite someone to a project (conversation):

1. Open the project you want to share
2. Click the **collaborators icon** in the project header
3. Enter the collaborator's email address
4. Click **Add**

The collaborator receives an email invitation with a link. When they click it, they are added to the project and can see the full conversation history and workspace.

## Inviting during project creation

When you create a new project (click **+** next to an agent), the creation form includes an **Invite collaborators** section. Add email addresses before creating the project so collaborators have access from the start.

## What collaborators see

Once added, collaborators see:

- The full conversation history
- Real-time streaming of new messages and agent responses
- Access to the agent's workspace files (based on their role)
- The names of other collaborators on messages they sent

## Cross-organization collaboration

You can invite people from outside your organization. When someone from a different org accepts an invitation, they get a temporary context in your org for that specific project. They do not gain access to your other agents or data.

## Removing collaborators

Open the collaborators dialog and click the **trash icon** next to a collaborator's name to remove their access. They will no longer be able to view or contribute to the project.

## Agent-level sharing

In addition to sharing individual projects, you can share an entire agent. Agent-level collaborators can see all projects they have access to and create new ones. Use this when you want someone to have ongoing access rather than access to a single conversation.
