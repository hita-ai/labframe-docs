---
title: Account Settings
description: Update your profile, change appearance, and manage your account.
---

Go to **Settings** from the profile menu in the bottom-left of the sidebar (click your avatar, then **Account settings**).

## Profile

The **Account** tab shows your profile information:

- **Name** — editable. Click **Save name** to update.
- **Email** — read-only. This is the email associated with your LabFrame account.

## Appearance

Choose your preferred theme:

| Option | Behavior |
|--------|----------|
| **Light** | Always uses the light theme |
| **Dark** | Always uses the dark theme |
| **System** | Follows your operating system's light/dark preference |

The theme picker is available both in Settings and in the profile menu under **Appearance**.

## Signing out

Click **Sign out** at the bottom of the Account tab, or from the profile menu in the sidebar. You will be redirected to the login page.

## Session expiration

If your session expires while you are using LabFrame, a dialog appears:

> *Your sign-in session expired. Reauthenticate to continue without losing your current route and saved draft state.*

Click **Sign in again** to reauthenticate. Your current page and any unsent draft message are preserved.
