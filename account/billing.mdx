---
title: Billing & Credits
description: How credits work, subscription plans, and top-ups.
---

LabFrame uses a credit-based billing system. Every AI interaction consumes credits from your account balance.

## Credits

Credits are consumed by:

- **Messages** — each message to the agent costs credits based on the model and response length
- **Tool usage** — commands, web searches, and file operations
- **Compute** — the agent's cloud VM has a small per-minute cost while active

Different models cost different amounts. More capable models (like Opus 4.6) use more credits per message than faster models (like Sonnet 4.6).

## Plans

Go to **Settings > Subscription** to see available plans. Each plan includes a monthly credit allowance:

| Plan | Credits/month | Best for |
|------|:-:|---------|
| **Starter** | 2,000 | Individual users, light usage |
| **Pro** | 4,000 | Active researchers and students |
| **Team** | 20,000 | Research labs and course sections |
| **Enterprise** | 50,000 | Departments and large deployments |

Toggle between **Monthly** and **Annual** billing at the top of the page. Annual billing saves up to 15%.

## Current balance

The **Current balance** card on the Subscription page shows:

- Your credit balance
- **Added this month** — credits from your plan
- **Spent this month** — credits used so far
- **Subscription** status (Active, Trialing, etc.)

If your balance is low, a **Low balance** badge appears. If you run out, a **Top up required** badge appears and your agents pause until credits are replenished.

The sidebar also shows a balance warning when credits are running low.

## Top-ups

If you need more credits before your next billing cycle, scroll down to **Top up credits** on the Subscription page. One-time credit packs are available in several sizes.

Top-up credits are added immediately and do not expire at the end of the month.

## Managing your subscription

Click **Manage subscription** to open the Stripe customer portal where you can:

- Change your plan
- Update payment method
- View invoices
- Cancel your subscription
