## Sign in

Go to [labframe.ai](https://labframe.ai) and click **Get started**. Enter your email address — LabFrame will send you a magic link. Click the link in your inbox and you are signed in. No password needed.

<Info>If your organization uses SSO, you may be redirected to your university or company login page instead.</Info>

## Create an agent

Click **+ New Agent** in the sidebar. Enter a name — something like "Research Assistant" or "Course Helper" — and select a machine type. Click **Create**.

LabFrame provisions a cloud VM for your agent. You will see status messages while it starts up:

- *Creating VM...*
- *Starting VM...*

Once the green dot appears next to your agent's name in the sidebar, the agent is ready.

## Send your first message

Type a request in the chat input at the bottom of the screen. For example:

> Build me a one-page portfolio site with a dark theme.

Press **Enter** (or click the send button) to submit.

Your agent will start working. You will see:

- A **thinking** indicator while the agent plans its approach
- **Tool activity** summaries as it writes files, runs commands, or searches the web (collapsed by default — click to expand)
- The **final response** with text and file links

## Open a deliverable

If your agent produced a file, it appears as a clickable link in the chat message — a small pill-shaped badge with the file name.

- **Click a `.webapp` link** to open a deployed website in a new tab
- **Click a `.pdf` link** to preview the PDF inline
- **Click any other file** to open it in the workspace file preview

You can also switch to the **Workspace** tab at any time to browse all the files your agent has created.

## Keep iterating

Send follow-up messages to refine the result. Your agent remembers the entire conversation and has access to all the files it already created.

> Make the hero section larger and add a contact form.

The agent edits the existing files and rebuilds.
